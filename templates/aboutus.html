About Us

Welcome to Byte Squad.

At ByteSquad, it’s not just about coding. It’s about kids inspiring kids, building together, and discovering what’s possible when imagination meets technology. Through fun, interactive sessions using Scratch, we guide kids in creating their own games, stories, and simple apps. Our approach is hands-on and playful, making coding feel less like “schoolwork” and more like an adventure
