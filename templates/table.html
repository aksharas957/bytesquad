About Us

Welcome!

At ByteSquad, it's not just about coding. It's about kids inspiring kids, building together, and discovering what's possible when imagination meets technology.

Our Mission

Through fun, interactive sessions using Scratch, we guide kids in creating their own games, stories, and simple apps. Our approach is hands-on and playful, making coding feel less like "schoolwork" and more like an adventure.

Our Organization

SoCal Tamil Youth Leadership and Entrepreneurship (STYLE) is an initiative of the SoCal Tamil Organization that empowers middle school, high school, and college students through leadership development and entrepreneurial thinking. Since its launch in 2018, STYLE has supported youth-driven initiatives and programs, including the creation of the ByteSquad Coding Club, providing students with opportunities to develop technical skills, collaborate on projects, and grow as innovative leaders.
